Concerta & Wellbeing

Wellbeing score (−5 to +5) is derived from self-reported focus ratings, task completion, energy levels, and keyword sentiment from session notes. It is a composite signal, not a clinical measure.

Zero-dose days correlate with being significantly upset, sick, on weekends, or during a deliberate tolerance break. Gaps are meaningful, not accidental.
